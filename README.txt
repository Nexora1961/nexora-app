Nexora PWA Quick Setup
======================

Files in this pack:
- /manifest.webmanifest
- /sw.js
- /icons/nexora-512.png
- /icons/nexora-192.png
- /icons/apple-touch-icon.png
- /head-snippet.html  (paste contents into your site's <head>)
- /body-snippet.html  (paste contents before </body>)

Steps (GoDaddy or any hosting):
1. Upload manifest.webmanifest to your site root (/)
2. Upload sw.js to your site root (/)
3. Create /icons folder at your site root and upload the three PNGs
4. Paste head-snippet.html into your site's <head> (Site Settings → Custom Code / Head)
5. Paste body-snippet.html before </body>
6. Publish the site

Test:
- Android (Chrome): Visit site → you should see "Install app" or use Chrome menu → "Install app"
- iPhone (Safari): Share → Add to Home Screen

Branding:
- Replace the placeholder icons with your official logo at the same filenames when ready.
